About Us - Litifer, The Journey of Playgpl

At Litifer, we are a progressive group of IIT Alumni, associated with the single vision of bringing the exciting world of Fantasy Sports and its delightful flavors to every corner of the globe.

We take immense pride in providing a global outreach, connecting enthusiasts and sports aficionados worldwide through our innovative platform. With our in-house tech team, we are at the forefront of crafting the future of fantasy gaming, ensuring a seamless and thrilling experience for our users.

In a world where playing games has become a new sport, we believe in fostering a vibrant recreational community, where every user is not just a player but a valued member of our extended family.

Today, Playgpl.com stands tall with over 200,000 app installs and is growing at an astonishing rate, doubling month-over-month. Our journey is a testament to our dedication, the trust our users place in us, and the boundless potential of the fantasy gaming world.

Join us on this exhilarating adventure. Let's play, let's connect, and let's make gaming more than just a pastime.
